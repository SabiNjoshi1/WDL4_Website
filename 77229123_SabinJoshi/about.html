About Us

We are a local trekking agency registered by the Government of Nepal with the register number: 00005/074/075 having decades of experience in trekking and expeditions in Nepal.
We recruit trained and experienced staff who have been involved for many years in the trekking and tour market. For all these years, our customers' health, protection, comfort, and satisfaction have been our top priority. We are the firmest name in sports activities such as trekking, mountaineering, travel, mountain flight, rafting and other outdoor adventures.
We are not only well known for providing the Trekking groups with a high level of service, but our interest is also to understand social justice and ecological responsibility. We know not only how important it is to conserve our cultural and natural heritage. Indeed, we have been cleaning campgrounds behind us for many years already-according to our slogan: leaving nothing but happiness behind.

Mission Statement:

To provide our customers with high quality service and meet their needs for a happy vacation in Nepal. To show the sense of duty of our Organization about local people's social concerns as well as other aspects of good sustainable practices.
